    about

    In an age of digital maximalism, this project explores the emerging discourse of softer computing, tracing its historical, social, and technological roots. It contributes to a growing, communal practice of care and knowledge, sharing by responding to technologies that prioritise agency, reflection, and ethical interaction. This small, personal investigation reimagines how we engage with the software we use as creatives and graphic designers, opening space to envision more adaptable, transparent, and open-source futures.
